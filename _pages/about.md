I'm Saron, a senior undergraduate Computer Science student at Stanford University. 


Research 🚀
======

1. **Video Event Retrieval:**
Developed a multilingual video-based event retrieval framework, achieving a 20-point nDCG@10 improvement. Trained XLM-R for task specific reranking and integrated state-of-the-art models to enhance cross-modal retrieval across diverse video content, achieving a 16-point nDCG@10 improvement.

2. **Afaan Oromoo Langauge Processing:**
Developed a spell-checker and word generation model for Afaan Oromoo, achieving 57% accuracy and 63% precision in predicting misspellings. Implemented a logistic regression model to predict the likelihood of word misspellings based on extended sounds, contributing to language standardization efforts.

3. **Multilingual Recursive Summarization Trees:**
Implemented and evaluated MuRST for multilingual question answering, using recursive summarization trees and hard clustering, achieving a 3-8% improvement in F1 and BLEU scores across languages. Developed custom clustering algorithms for hierarchical summarization, enhancing QA accuracy for long-form documents.[Poster](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1XpTWYwMf4o6yhPb7HFD-jliCH-PW55jv/view?usp=sharing) [Paper](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Z26GxzuSHgPuWdnfF0xBISTXaWfjufIp/view?usp=sharing)

4. **Prompts as Auto-Optimized Training Hyperparameters:** 
Developed reranking methods within the DSPy framework, which were utilized in training neural retrieval models with minimal gold labels achieving a 4.5-point improvement in nDCG@10 over larger models using optimized LM-generated queries, outperforming RankZephyr and RankLLaMA in BIRCO benchmarks. [Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2406.11706)
